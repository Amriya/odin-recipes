Coffee

Description

A coffee is an energizing drink that can be a great starter to your day.

It keeps you fresh and energized.

Ingredients:

  • Coffee powder
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Fill the cup with one spoon coffee powder and sugar as per taste
  2. Fill the cup with hot milk.
  3. Stir and enjoy the drink hot.
