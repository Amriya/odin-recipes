Tea

Description

An energizing drink that can be enjoyed when served hot.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Tea Powder
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. For one cup of tea, boil 2 tea spoons of tea powder with milk.
  2. Let it boil till milk changes color.
  3. Strain the tea into a cup, add sugar as per taste and stir.
